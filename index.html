Convoy 2020

A social and medical convoy

This year’s experience with the Social Members Club of the National Institute of Post and Transport was really different from all the experiences that I had previously passed on, because it combined between continuous hard work and between pleasure and fun.

Olympiades INPT 2020

An innovation for your entertaiment. A reinvention of our tradition

Having a great reputation in terms of quality, the INPT Olympiads, organized by the Association of Engineering Students of the National Institute of Post and Telecommunications (AEINPT), are the annual meeting of more than 7000 students from different schools and universities.

Gathered on an exceptional site on one of the most prestigious schools in Morocco, the Olympics of the INPT are clearly the stronghold of the entertainment known within the community of engineering students. This can never be a coincidence, especially since the program of the Olympiads is rich in activities; sports and cultural competitions, outdoor shows, a musical evening and many other surprises in a special student atmosphere. These highlight the talents of our dear participants and guarantee an atmosphere of enjoyment.

And as the INPT goes hand in hand with digital and aims to contribute to the development of the digital ecosystem in a dynamic of continuous transformation, we chose as the theme of this edition: "Digital in the reinvention of entertainment in era of innovation ”.
